Discrimination


Discriminatory abuse occurs when individuals have not been treated equally in line with legislation.

It means that a person’s values, beliefs and cultures are ignored, and individuals may be harassed or degraded due to a protected characteristic such as religion or disability.

Signs and symptoms include:

  • Poor care and support which does not meet the individual’s needs.
  • Verbal abuse.
  • Disrespect towards the individual.
  • Exclusion from activities or services.
  • Isolation.
