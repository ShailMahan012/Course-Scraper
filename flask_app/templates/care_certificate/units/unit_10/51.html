Summary/reflection


You have now completed the final section. You have learnt:

  • Most disclosures will take place verbally when employees are speaking with individuals and this can be in any circumstance at all.
  • Employees should be prepared for allegations of abuse to take place at any time when they are working with vulnerable adults.
  • Within a health and social care setting the care worker will be initially responsible for making a report to their line manager.
  • The most important thing that any employee should keep in mind if someone makes a disclosure to them is that they must not ensure complete confidentiality to the individual.
  • Most organisations’ code of practice will include the four key priorities that must be followed when responding to allegations of abuse: protect, report and refer, preserve and record.
