Summary of Care Certificate Standard 2

You have now completed the Care Certificate Standard 2, you have covered a number of points including:

Personal development plans

You now know that your employer will create your personal development plan and this should be updated and reviewed annually.

Developing knowledge and skills

You have learnt that internal and external opportunities will allow you to develop your skills and knowledge and in turn will help you achieve your objectives.

Setting SMART targets

You have explored the four steps to setting SMART objectives in detail. You now know SMART targets will help you achieve your objectives.

Receiving regular feedback

You have learnt receiving regular feedback will help you develop your skills, provide clarity and provide you with the ability to meet the required standards.

Developing your training

You have learnt that it is vital that you develop your training and qualifications further as a health and social care worker, this can be achieved by CPD training.
