Introduction


Standard 13, Health and Safety is one of the 15 standards you must complete to achieve your Care Certificate.

This standard has been divided into four sections:

  • What is meant by health and safety and why it’s needed.
  • Health and safety legislation and regulations.
  • Employee and employers responsibilities and risk assessments.
  • Essential workplace practices.
