Summary/reflection


You have now completed this section. You have learnt:

  • Regardless of what your role is in health and social care, or the type of workplace you work in, it is crucial to have the correct core literacy, numeracy and communication skills.
  • Good communication is one of the 6C’s. It is of vital importance that information is exchanged, accurate and not misleading.
  • You can check, and get help, with your literacy, numeracy and communication skills on a variety of websites on the internet. There are also materials available offline and face-to-face learning opportunities.
  • You should discuss further opportunities, resources and the support available with your manager.
