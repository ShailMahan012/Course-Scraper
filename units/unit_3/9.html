Summary/reflection


You have now completed the first section. You have learnt:

  • As a health and social care worker, you have a duty of care to all individuals who you are caring for and supporting and to other workers.
  • Duty of care is not optional; it is a legal requirement. If you break your duty of care, it could result in legal action.
  • The duty of care is part of the Code of Conduct. It details the behaviours that are expected of you as a health and social care worker.
  • Different workplaces will have different agreed ways of working. You will need to check them when you move to a new job in health and social care.
  • If you have any concerns about wellbeing in your workplace, you should raise them, as part of your duty of care.
