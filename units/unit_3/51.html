Summary of Care Certificate Standard 3

You have now completed the Care Certificate Standard 3, you have covered a number of points including:

What duty of care involves

You have learnt that duty of care involves promoting people's rights, protecting people from harm, abuse and injury and promoting people's wellbeing.

Complaints legislation

You have explored that the legislation for complaints in health and social care is the Local Authority Social Services and NHS Complaints (England) Regulations 2009.

Mistakes

You have considered the reasons why mistakes happen, this includes stress, poor communication between workers, a lack of worker knowledge or understanding.

Relevant Legislation

You have explored the relevant legislation that applies to health and social care, this includes the health and safety at work act 1974, RIDDOR, COSHH and PUWER.

Factors that cause conflict

You have learnt that there are a number of factors that can cause conflict, including biological, social, environmental and psychological factors.
