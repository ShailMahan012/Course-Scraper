Learning outcomes


After completing this Care Certificate 13, you should:

  • Have an understanding of how health and safety applies in care roles.
  • Understand what your responsibilities are.
  • Understand workplace stress and how to tackle it.

The Care Certificate Standards detail what you must achieve and be assessed against to meet these learning outcomes.

If you have any concerns or queries, you should discuss these with your employer and/or assessor.
