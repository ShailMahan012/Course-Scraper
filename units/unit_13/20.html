Summary/reflection


You have now this section. You have learnt:

  • The HSWA applies to all industries and workplaces in Great Britain and can also include offshore installations such as oil rigs.
  • The Management of Health and Safety at Work Regulations (MHSWR) 1999 places a duty on employers, employees and others.
  • The Fire Safety Order (2005) was designed to outline the responsibilities of different people with relation to fire safety.
  • The aim of the COSHH Regulations is to protect the health of workers, and others, who could come into contact with hazardous substances in the workplace.
  • PPE must be provided if there is a risk of injury/ill health, and there is no other way of controlling the risks.
