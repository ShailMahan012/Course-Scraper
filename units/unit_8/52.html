Summary/reflection


You have now completed the final section. You have learnt:

  • An assessment should be made regarding an individual’s nutrition and hydration when they are receiving any type of care or support.
  • You must work in person-centred ways to ensure that any food and drink provided is suitable and meets the individual’s unique needs.
  • Do not make assumptions about an individual’s beliefs or preferences. Always ask the individual about their specific needs.
  • Compassion is one of the 6Cs. This means that all individuals should be treated in a compassionate way to help them maintain sufficient nutrition and hydration. Their needs should always be respected.
  • The individual’s needs and choices must be met wherever possible, and their care plan is a crucial part of recording and delivering this.
  • If you have any concerns regarding an individual’s fluid intake, or their food or nutrition, you should report to your manager, a senior staff member, their carer or their family.
